Instructions to the Uno game:


Starting the game:

1. In the top left corner of the screen you can:
    - set the amount of players (2-6)
    - set the starting amount of cards in hand (1-20)
    - start the game by pressing New Game button

2. On the right top corner, there is an exit button



How the game works:

1. Each player receives x amount of cards

2. The goal of the game is to get rid of all the cards
    - winner is the player who gets rid of them first

3. You can only play same type cards (color or type)
    - you can place a green color on green
    - you can place a blue five on red five
    - you can NOT place a blue five on red six
    - you can also place multiple cards that meet the above

4. To play a card, select it in your hand (bottom middle) and click "Play cards" button

5. If you do not have needed cards, you can draw new cards from the deck.
    - click on the upside card in the middle of the screen
    - you can take 3 cards from the deck
    - if after taking 3 cards you still do not have needed cards,
      click on the deck again and the turn will be given to the next player.

Special cards:

Take two:
    - next player takes 2 cards

Take four:
    - next player takes 4 cards
    - color of the played card is randomly chosen

Wild:
    - color of the played card is randomly chosen

Skip:
    - skips the turn of the next player
    - if played x amount of this card, skips x amount of players

Reverse:
    - reverses the game direction
    - note. in game of two it basically does nothing


Additional notes about UI:

After selecting the first card, you can only select cards that match its type.

In order to skip turn, you need to take 3 cards and click on the deck icon again.

You can select/unselect cards by clicking them.

In order to play cards, you need to select them and click "Play cards" button.

Additional game info will be shown just above the deck icon. (player turn, errors)
